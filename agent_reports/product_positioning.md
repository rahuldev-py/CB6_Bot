```

    → 7-day free Starter trial (dashboard + paper signals)
```

Key onboarding principles:
1. **No credit card for free trial** — friction kills trust
2. **First value within 5 minutes** — daily report delivered immediately on join
3. **Telegram-first** — India's traders live on Telegram, not email
4. **Education before pitch** — first 3 messages must educate, not sell
