## The Core Concept

Today's NIFTY trade and every A+ Forex trade share the **exact same ICT structure**.
Markets are different. The pattern is universal.

**The 5-step sequence — works on every liquid market:**

```
Step 1: IDENTIFY DOL (Draw on Liquidity)
        → Cluster of stop losses sitting above/below recent swing
        → Price will go hunt these stops before the real move

Step 2: SWEEP THE DOL (Liquidity Grab)
        → Price wicks through the DOL level
        → Candle CLOSES back — this is NOT a breakout, it is a TRAP
        → Retail traders get stopped out / enter wrong direction

Step 3: ORDER BLOCK FORMS (Smart Money Loading)
        → Price consolidates after the sweep — does NOT continue
        → 3+ candles in tight range = smart money accumulating
        → Longer OB duration = more conviction = higher quality

Step 4: BOS / CHoCH (Structure Shift Confirmed)
        → Large displacement candle closes beyond the last swing
        → THIS is the confirmation signal — not before this candle
        → Displacement body must be 3x+ average candle body

Step 5: FVG ENTRY (Precision Entry)
        → Displacement candle leaves a gap (Fair Value Gap)
        → Price retraces into FVG = smart money re-loading at discount
        → Entry = FVG bottom (LONG) or FVG top (SHORT)
        → SL = below sweep wick (LONG) or above sweep wick (SHORT) + buffer
```
