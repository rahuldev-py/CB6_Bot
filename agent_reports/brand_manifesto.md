## The Belief System

### 1. Markets reward preparation, not prediction.
Most traders try to predict price. CB6 traders prepare for setups. There is a difference. Prediction is ego. Preparation is discipline.

### 2. Risk management is the alpha, not the signal.
A 60% win rate with no risk control is a slow bleed. A 52% win rate with hard drawdown limits and position sizing is a business. CB6 builds businesses, not gambling habits.

### 3. Discipline is a system, not a personality trait.
You cannot willpower your way out of a losing streak at 3:15pm when you're already down ₹12,000. CB6 removes the decision from the emotional state. Kill switches don't care how you feel.

### 4. Every trade is a data point.
A bad trade is not a failure. It is information. CB6 journals every trade, tags every setup, tracks every outcome. The ML models learn. The system improves. The trader improves.

### 5. The market is not your enemy. Your emotions are.
FOMO enters. Fear exits. Greed holds too long. Ego adds to losers. CB6 was built because the market structure is readable — the human execution of it is not. CB6 bridges that gap.

### 6. Prop firms are the fastest path for undercapitalized talent.
A trader with ₹26,000 personal capital is limited. A trader with a GFT $5K funded account and proper discipline can withdraw real money in 30 days. CB6 was built to serve this exact path.

### 7. Intelligence compounds.
Every trade logged is a lesson stored. Every ML cycle is a model improved. Every week of discipline is a week of proof built. CB6 compounds intelligence the way markets compound money.

## Visual Identity Principles

- Dark mode by default (trading terminal standard)
- Data-dense but organized (traders expect information, not whitespace)
- Mobile-optimized first — most Indian traders are on phones
