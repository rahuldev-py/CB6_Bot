## The 9 Revenue Lines

### 1. SaaS Subscriptions (Primary, Years 1–5)
**Revenue share target:** 50–60% of total

- Starter: ₹999/month → $29/month
- Pro: ₹2,999/month → $79/month
- Elite: ₹7,999–₹14,999/month → $199–$499/month
- Annual plans at 20–25% discount (improves cash flow + retention)

**Key metric:** Monthly churn must stay below 5% (industry benchmark for B2B SaaS is 3–5%)

**Retention levers:**
- Weekly reports keep users engaged even in low-activity periods
- Setup grading creates habit loop (grade → journal → improve)
- Community creates switching cost
- Annual plan discount locks in cash
